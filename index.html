Onion Export Data from India

India is a major onion exporter, with 819.8K shipments in 2023-24. The top export destinations include Sri Lanka, Malaysia, and the United Arab Emirates. The government has lifted export restrictions, including the 20% duty, effective April 1, 2025, according to The Economic Times and PIB. Onion Export Data: 2023-24: India shipped 819.8K onion shipments, with 14,005 exporters and 31,075 buyers. Top Destinations: Sri Lanka, Malaysia, and the United Arab Emirates. Leading Exporter: India is the world's largest onion exporter, followed by China and the Netherlands.
